download video:
yt-dlp "url"

get thumbnail of a video:
yt-dlp --write-thumbnail --skip-download "https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID"

get frames of a video
ffmpeg -i input.mp4 frame_%04d.png

/\
|
then do stuff to the frames, brighten and stuff

make audio file from video:
ffmpeg -i input.mp4 -vn -c:a mp3 output.mp3

make spectrogram:
ffmpeg -i output.mp3 -lavfi showspectrumpic=s=1280x720:color=rainbow spectrogram.png
